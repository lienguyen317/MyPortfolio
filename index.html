About Me

Self motivated full-stack developer with the eagerness and ability to acquire new and challenging concepts. A forward thinker, who isn’t afraid to experiment with contemporary ideas to resolve current issues and creatively discover different solutions to enhance society at large. I am a logical thinker with the courage to take on any challenges to aid myself and others in our continuous journey of changes and improvements.

My Hobby

Located in San Francisco, I enjoy the beauty of the city and appreciate the diversified cultures and architectural structures that it has to offered. As the needs to explore grew, I ventured out to different locations in and out of the U.S. So far, I've traveled to Singapore, Japan, Vietnam (my birth country), and Myanmar. I plan to veture out and explore many more countries in the future and continue to expand my knowledge of the world as a whole.

Photography Interest

As I traveled, I found photography to be intriguing. I am not a professional photographer by any mean. However, I love to capture the beauty of places I've been to and share it with my family and friends.
"A picture is worth a thousand words" - Fred R. Barnard.
